Coaching Cues Reference

Quick reference cues to remember during practice. Use these to help Jayden focus on key technique points!

üèä
Freestyle
  • "Reach and roll" - extend and rotate shoulders
  • "Push water past your pocket" - finish each stroke
  • "Fingertip drag" - high elbow recovery
  • "Head down, hips up" - body position
  • "Grab and throw" - powerful catch
üîô
Backstroke
  • "Pinky first in, thumb first out"
  • "Belly button to the sky"
  • "Paint the ceiling" - arm recovery
  • "Rotate like a rotisserie chicken"
  • "Kick from your hips, not knees"
üê∏
Breaststroke
  • "Heart shape" - pull pattern
  • "Heels to butt, snap together, glide"
  • "Hide behind your hands" - recovery
  • "Pull, breathe, kick, glide" - timing
  • "Shoot forward like a torpedo"
ü¶ã
Butterfly
  • "Throw your hands forward, not down"
  • "Press your chest, pop your hips"
  • "Dolphin from your chest"
  • "Big kick in, small kick out"
  • "Eyes down on entry"
üîÑ
Turns
  • "Tight ball, fast spin" - flip turns
  • "Touch-tuck-explode" - open turns
  • "Push off on your side" - free/back
  • "Streamline is free speed!"
  • "5 dolphin kicks minimum"
üèÅ
Finishes
  • "Head down, drive through!" - FR/BK
  • "Lunge at the wall!" - BR/FL
  • "No gliding into the wall"
  • "Sprint through the touch"
  • "Two hands, same time" - BR/FL
