Quick Reference Cues

One cue at a time, repeat consistently

Freestyle

  • Reach and roll
  • Push water past pocket
  • Fingertip drag
  • Head down, hips up

Backstroke

  • Pinky in, thumb out
  • Belly button to sky
  • Paint the ceiling
  • Kick from hips

Breaststroke

  • Heart shape pull
  • Heels to butt, snap
  • Hide behind hands
  • Shoot like torpedo

Butterfly

  • Throw hands forward
  • Press chest, pop hips
  • Dolphin from chest
  • Big kick in, small out

Turns

  • Tight ball, fast spin
  • Touch-tuck-explode
  • Push off on side
  • 5 dolphin kicks min

Finishes

  • Head down, drive!
  • Lunge at wall!
  • No gliding in
  • Sprint through touch
