Phase 1: Foundation

Week 1: FREESTYLE
Fins
Snorkel
Kickboard
Pull Buoy
10 minWARM-UP
4 x 50 Choice swim - easy, long strokes
4 x 25 Kick (no board) - streamline, tight core
Cue: "Squeeze belly button to spine"
10 minKICK DEVELOPMENT
With fins: 4 x 50 Flutter kick on back - hands at sides
With kickboard: 4 x 25 Breaststroke kick - pause 2 sec in streamline
20 minSTROKE TECHNIQUEFreestyle
With snorkel: 4 x 50 Catch-up drill (thumb to thumb)
4 x 25 Fingertip drag (high elbow recovery)
4 x 50 6-kick switch drill (6 kicks side, 3 strokes, switch)
4 x 25 Fist drill (feel water with forearms)
4 x 25 Full stroke - COUNT STROKES and write down
Cue: "Reach and roll - long strokes, rotate shoulders"
10 minTURN STATION
8 x 25 with FAST turns (flip: tight ball, fast spin)
4 x wall push-offs into streamline, hold for 5 kicks
Cue: "Be a torpedo - tight and straight"
10 minCOOL DOWN
200 easy choice + 4 x 25 drill of choice
Total: 60 minutes

Quick Reference | Coaching Cues and Tracking

Coaching Cues

Freestyle

  • "Reach and roll"
  • "Push water past pocket"
  • "Fingertip drag"
  • "Head down, hips up"

Backstroke

  • "Pinky in, thumb out"
  • "Belly button to sky"
  • "Paint the ceiling"
  • "Kick from hips"

Breaststroke

  • "Heart shape" pull
  • "Heels to butt, snap"
  • "Hide behind hands"
  • "Shoot like torpedo"

Butterfly

  • "Throw hands forward"
  • "Press chest, pop hips"
  • "Dolphin from chest"
  • "Big kick in, small out"

Turns

  • "Tight ball, fast spin"
  • "Touch-tuck-explode"
  • "Push off on side"
  • "5 dolphin kicks min"

Finishes

  • "Head down, drive!"
  • "Lunge at wall!"
  • "No gliding in"
  • "Sprint through touch"

Stroke Count and Tempo Tracking

Distances: Freestyle = 50, 100, 200 | Backstroke, Breaststroke, Butterfly = 50, 100
